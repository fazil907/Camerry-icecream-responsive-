About us

The perfect blend of freshness, quality, and taste.

Indulge in heavenly scoops of real fruits, rich creamy milk and lip-smacking natural flavours. Lose yourself in pleasure. Our ice creams are like comfortable hugs for your taste buds.

Every morning, tanker loads of fresh milk is brought in fresh from a few select dairies. Fresh seasonal and exotic fruits arrive in parallel and are carefully cleaned, scooped and chopped to just the right sizes or turned into fresh pulp. Multiple ultra-modern and state-of-the-art, hygienic plants then get to work and turn the recipes created by our Ice Cream Chefs into those delectable dollops of ice creams that you indulge in.

Our Ice Cream crazy chefs are the stars. They are fanatical about using only freshest ingredients like rich creamy milk, exotic fruits, nuts and only natural flavours. They are constantly trying various permutations and combinations till the next Eureka moment. Once there it’s a frenzied race to get it quickly to you, so go ahead tell them about your experience every time you try a new Camerry Ice Cream. They are waiting to hear from you.
